Welcome to Find A Game! coded by James Grodzicki, Ahmed Ahmed, and Fahad Alam for our Bootcamp Project 1. This site utilizes the OpenWeather API to display the temperature of a user-selected zipcode, and then suggests a videogame based on the result. The game data is powered by the GiantBomb API.

Start by entering your 5-digit zipcode into the Search Box!
